Visit our new restaurant Gyoza King opened in Kluuvi!

A new gyoza restaurant, Gyoza King, has opened on the second floor of Kluuvi. The restaurant offers tasty, The restaurant offers tasty, Japanese-inspired, handmade dumplings. The restaurant has attracted interest among Kluuvi visitors even before its opening and is a welcome addition to Kluuvi's diverse restaurant offering.

We have noticed that dumplings are popular in Finland. However, we haven't found any that we think are good enough and actually taste homemade. We wanted to try making them ourselves, we succeeded, and that's how the concept for our newest restaurant, Gyoza King, was born. We are really happy that we were able to open our new restaurant in Kluuvi!" commented the restaurant owners.

Menu

Gyoza King's menu includes gyoza in classic flavors such as pork & cabbage, chicken & leek, and tofu, corn & mushroom. In addition to gyoza dishes, Japanese-style curry rice dishes are also available in both meat and vegetarian options.
